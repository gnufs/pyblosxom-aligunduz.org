Snapshots from a GSM Workshop
<p>
Recently, I attended the <a href="http://openbts.sourceforge.net/">OpenBTS</a> workshop organized by <a href="http://www.kestrelsp.com/team.html">David Burgess</a>. The 4-day workshop was hosted by <a href="http://www.mirider.com/">Dieter Spaar</a> in his lovely Bavarian farm. Beside the many participants from across the globe with a variety of backgrounds, <a href="http://gnumonks.org/users/laforge/">Harald Welte</a> and <a href="http://www.cs.virginia.edu/~kn5f/">Karsten Nohl</a> were also in attendance. I won't get into much detail about the covered material since I'm sure they will be properly summarized in the <a href="http://openbts.blogspot.com/">OpenBTS Blog</a> soon. Instead, I will just share some snapshots.
</p>
